Services

Comprehensive medical services tailored for you

Neurology Services

Our neurology team provides comprehensive care for disorders of the brain and nervous system. From diagnostic EEGs and advanced neuroimaging to personalized treatment plans for conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, Parkinson’s, and multiple sclerosis, we combine the latest technology with compassionate expertise to help you achieve optimal neurological health.

Cardiology Services

Our cardiology specialists offer advanced diagnostics and treatments for all heart conditions. From non-invasive stress tests and echocardiograms to interventional procedures such as angioplasty, our team delivers personalized cardiac care to keep your heart healthy and strong.

Pediatrics Services

We provide compassionate, family-centered pediatric care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our services include well-child exams, immunizations, developmental screenings, and management of acute and chronic childhood illnesses to help your child thrive at every stage.

Orthopedic Services

Our orthopedic experts diagnose and treat a full range of musculoskeletal issues, from sports injuries to degenerative joint diseases. We offer minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures, joint replacement surgery, and tailored rehabilitation programs to restore your mobility and quality of life.

Radiology Services

Equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technology, our radiology department delivers precise diagnostic studies including MRI, CT, ultrasound, and X-ray. Our board-certified radiologists collaborate closely with your care team to provide accurate interpretations that guide effective treatment plans.

Gastroenterology Services

Our gastroenterology clinic specializes in the diagnosis and management of digestive disorders. We perform endoscopic procedures, liver function tests, and dietary consultations to address conditions such as IBS, GERD, hepatitis, and bowel diseases, helping you achieve optimal gastrointestinal health.
